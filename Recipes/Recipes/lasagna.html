Lasagna

Homemade lasagna made in 3 hours, it is very cheap and tasty

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Sugar
  • Spices
  • Egg
  • Cheese
  • Lasanga noodles

Steps

  1. Layer the meat sauce
  2. Layer the noodles
  3. Layer the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the cheese
  5. Layer the meat sauce
  6. Layer the cheese
  7. Cook at 350 degrees for 25 minutes
